*** WLAN & WIFI

WLAN is intended for “local” use (the “L” in WLAN). Also referred to as Wi-Fi, it's probably the most common way to wirelessly connect to the Internet. WWAN is a mobile broadband3 option that covers a “wide” area (the “W” in WWAN). You may have heard it called a “3G” or in some areas a “4G” network.
