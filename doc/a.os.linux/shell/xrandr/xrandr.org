* xrandr

The full name for RANDR is X Resize, Rotate, and Reflect Extension.

这个字母代表着r and r

Xrandr - X Resize, Rotate and Reflection extension.

xrandr, xrandr --prop and xrandr --verbose will give you some information with different level of detail.


xrandr  --fb  3200x2000 --output LVDS --scale 2.5x2.5 --output
              VGA              --pos              0x0              --panning
              3200x2000+0+0/3200x2000+0+0/64/64/64/64
